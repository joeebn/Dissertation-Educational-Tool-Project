Ethics Quiz

What is ethics in AI?

The study of how AI can be used to make ethical decisions
The development of AI systems that are inherently ethical
The study of how AI systems can impact society and how to address ethical concerns

What is bias in AI?

When an AI system intentionally discriminates against a particular group
When an AI system unintentionally discriminates against a particular group
When an AI system is too accurate and therefore unfair

What is transparency in AI?

The ability of an AI system to explain how it arrived at a particular decision
The ability of an AI system to be easily understood by non-experts
The ability of an AI system to learn from feedback

What is privacy in AI?

The ability of individuals to control their personal information in the context of AI systems
The ability of AI systems to keep sensitive data secure
The ability of AI systems to learn from large amounts of data

What is impartiality in AI?

The ability of an AI system to be accurate in its predictions
The ability of an AI system to treat all individuals equally
The ability of an AI system to learn from feedback

What is accountability in AI?

The ability of an AI system to be transparent in its decision-making process
The ability of an AI system to learn from feedback
The ability of humans to hold AI systems responsible for their actions

What is the role of government in regulating AI?

To ensure that AI systems are developed and used ethically and responsibly
To encourage the development of AI systems without restrictions
To provide funding for the development of AI systems

What is the responsibility of AI developers?

To ensure that AI systems are developed and used ethically and responsibly
To maximize profits at any cost
To develop AI systems that are as advanced and intelligent as possible
